UX/UI Design

This project consisted in the creation of a landing page for whatever product we pleased. This was a project conceived during the UX/UI Design IEFP training

Concept

For this project, I opted for creating a landing page for a crystal therapy workshop, conducted by the fictional store Mystic. Mystic is more than a shop, it is a space of connection, knowledge, and community. Its objective is to not only sell handmade and ethically obtained products, but also to create a community and offer various ways to learn about spirituality.

Personas

  • Lúcia is a 23 yo student who’s just now beginning her spirituality journey, in attempts to reduce her anxiety and find inner peace. Even though interested, she often finds difficult to obtain content fitted to her knowledge level, with overloaded platforms and confusing language
  • Celest is a 56yo Reiki Master who’s already a well known name in the area. Many times she finds herself frustrated with the lack of transparency when it comes to the origin of the products and whether they’re sustainable or not. She’s also in search of possible collaborations and of a larger community

Competitive Analysis

When analysing competitors, some of the strong suits were the offer of various services - like newsletters and workshops, as well as consultations -, the clear communication of values and mission, and videos on tips. However, some of the stores had an outdated and convoluted design, confusing information architecture, and lack of educational content

Lofi Wireframes

At this stage, I figured out what information would be relevant and designed where everything should be, in order to provide the necessary information clearly without overloading the user

Design System

Here I established the overall look of the page, defining colours, fonts, and spacing, to ensure everything is cohesive visually, and matched the shop’s identity. It was also important to guarantee compliance with the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, to make sure every user can easily access and understand the information on the landing page

Hifi Wireframes

Having a design system defined, I finally applied it to the structure previously designed, always having usability and accessibility in mind, ensuring a good user experience to all.
